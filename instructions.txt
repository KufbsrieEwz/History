1. Answer the questions: Click the answer you think is correct or press the corresponding key to try and gain a chance to roll!
2. Roll the die: Click the die or press space while there are arrows around it to roll the die. You will move forward depending on what you roll!
3. Continue play: Continue play until someone gets past the last tile, they are then deemed the winner!
4. Tiles:
  a. Plains: This is just a normal tile!
  b. Forest: Due to difficult terrain, you will move slightly slower when travelling through this tile!
  c. Tundra: Due to harsh terrain, you will move significantly slower than normal while travelling through this tile!
  d. Water: You cannot stop on this tile, you must either cross the entire body of water in one go or not cross it at all!
